## Why a Daily Blog?

Three reasons:

1. **Track progress** — I want to see how far I've come
2. **Share learnings** — Maybe others can learn from my mistakes
3. **Build in public** — Forces me to ship consistently

- **Desktop apps** — Windows Command Palette extensions
- **Hardware** — ESP32 projects (M5Timer is my latest!)
